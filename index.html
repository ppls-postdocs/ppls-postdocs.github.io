About Us

We are an independent network of postdoctoral researchers in the school of Philosophy, Psychology and Language Sciences (PPLS) at the University of Edinburgh. Our goal is to support the research, career development, and mental health of fixed term research and teaching employees in the school. We aim to represent the interests of postdoctoral researchers at both the school and subject area level by holding events and providing resources.

Follow us on Twitter or check out or list of periodical and recurring events to get involved. To join the network, contact ppls dot postdoc dot soc at gmail dot com

Events

We meet on the last Friday of every month for a drink, usually at Checkpoint on Bristo Place.

We also meet on the second Tuesday of every month at 10am for coffee and biscuits in the concourse of 7 George Square.

Feel free to pop by either of these events, even for only 10 minutes.
