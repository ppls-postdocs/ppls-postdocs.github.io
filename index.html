About Us

We are an independent network of Early Career Researchers (postdocs and teaching fellows) in the School of Philosophy, Psychology and Language Sciences (PPLS) at the University of Edinburgh. Our goal is to support the research, career development, and mental health of fixed term research and teaching employees in the School. We aim to represent the interests of postdoctoral researchers at both the school and subject area level by holding events and providing resources.

Follow us on Twitter or check out or list of periodical and recurring events to get involved. To join the network, contact ppls dot postdoc dot soc at gmail dot com

Part of our role as a support network is representing the interests of ECRs at the School level. This is done by two main Representatives, one on a mainly research contract (Dr Alexander Martin) and one on a mainly teaching contract (TBD). These Representatives are supported in their roles by three subject area Liaisons (Linguistics: Dr Mora Maldonado, Psychology: Dr Lucía Castillo, Philosophy: Dr Sophie Keeling). If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact your subject area Liaison or Representative.
